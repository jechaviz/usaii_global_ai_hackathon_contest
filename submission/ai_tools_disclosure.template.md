## Responsible AI Statement

The system supports student decision-making by explaining recommendation scores,
skill gaps, evidence requirements, and human approval gates. It does not infer
protected traits, does not guarantee scholarships or jobs, and does not make
final outreach, academic, or eligibility decisions without human review.
