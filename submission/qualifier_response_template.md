# USAII AI Readiness Qualifier Response Template

Team: AI Study-to-Work Coach

## Idea Summary

We are building an explainable AI coach that helps students convert interests and skill confidence into a responsible learning plan, portfolio artifact, and human-approved opportunity shortlist.

## AI Use

AI assists preference extraction, skill-gap reasoning, rubric generation, opportunity matching, and responsible AI critique. The demo exposes scores, gaps, guardrails, and approval gates.

## Responsible AI

We avoid protected-trait inference, use synthetic public personas, require human review before outreach, and clearly warn that recommendations support decisions rather than guarantee outcomes.

## Data Boundary

Public demo data is synthetic. Real student records, school proof, age verification, guardian contacts, and account credentials stay private and are never committed.

## Student Gate

Every team member must be a currently enrolled eligible student and registered on Devpost before the qualifier.

## Final Authority

Only the eligible student lead may perform final external submissions from an authenticated account.
