# Judge Demo Script

Use this when recording the final 3-5 minute video or presenting live.

## 0:00-0:25: Open With Proof

"This is AI Study-to-Work Coach. It turns student goals into a learning plan,
portfolio proof, and human-approved next action. The competitive difference is
that every recommendation has an evidence trail."

Show metrics:

- judge readiness 94;
- decision delta +29;
- risk cut 71%;
- final submit gated.

## 0:25-1:10: Coach Interaction

Open Coach tab.

Move one skill slider. Say:

"The score and gaps update because the coach compares student confidence against
the skills needed for each opportunity path."

## 1:10-1:55: Plan

Open Plan tab.

"The output is not just advice. It is a four-week proof sprint: decision
baseline, skill-gap sprint, portfolio artifact, and opportunity match."

## 1:55-3:00: Judge Mode

Open Judge tab.

"We map the official undergraduate rubric directly: 30% AI/analytics reasoning,
25% architecture, 20% problem understanding, 15% impact and 10% responsible AI."

Say:

"Against generic advice, the coach improved decision clarity from 57 to 86 and
reduced risky claims by 71% across five synthetic cases."

Open Evidence Graph:

"This is the architecture in judge-visible form: input, reasoning, output,
control, receipt."

## 3:00-3:50: Responsible AI

Open guardrails or mention:

- no protected-trait inference;
- no guarantees;
- synthetic public data;
- missing evidence is visible;
- outreach and public claims require student approval.

## 3:50-4:30: Submission Readiness

Open Packet tab.

"The qualifier and Devpost packet are prepared, but final submission remains
gated to an eligible student lead with a valid qualifier code."

## Closing Line

"AI Study-to-Work Coach makes student AI advice accountable: plan, proof,
guardrails and receipts."
