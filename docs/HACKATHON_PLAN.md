## Scoring Focus

- Problem understanding: name the decision context and constraints.
- AI/analytics reasoning: explain why AI is appropriate and how it is evaluated.
- Solution design: show data-to-AI-to-output flow.
- Impact: show how the student decision changes.
- Responsible AI: bias, hallucination, privacy, failure modes, and mitigation.
