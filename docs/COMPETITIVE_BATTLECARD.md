# Competitive Battlecard

## Winning Position

`AI Study-to-Work Coach` should not be presented as a career chatbot. The winning
frame is:

> Evidence-backed student decision support: plan, portfolio proof, human gates,
> and a judge-visible receipt trail.

## Why This Beats Common Submissions

| Competitor pattern | Likely strength | Weakness to exploit | Our counter |
|---|---|---|---|
| Generic chatbot | Fast demo, easy story | Advice without measurable decision change | Show baseline 57 -> coach 86 and evidence graph. |
| Resume/portfolio generator | Clear artifact | Starts after the student already knows what to build | Start earlier: choose the learning path and proof artifact. |
| No-code workflow | Polished UI | Thin architecture and weak responsible AI | Show V core, rubric model, risk reduction, WAIBAv gates. |
| Dataset dashboard | Looks analytical | May not change a student action | Show the decision, human gate, outreach-safe shortlist. |
| Overbuilt agent demo | Impressive but risky | Hard to explain, may hallucinate or overclaim | Make every recommendation traceable and reversible. |

## Judge Hook

"We measured the gap between generic AI advice and accountable student decision
support. Across five synthetic student cases, baseline advice scored 57, the
coach scored 86, and risky unsupported claims fell 71%."

## Demo Must-Haves

- Start on metrics: fit 84, judge readiness 94, decision delta +29, risk cut
  71%, final submit gated.
- Move one slider to prove the system is interactive, not a static mockup.
- Open Judge tab before Packet tab.
- Name the official rubric weights while showing the score bars.
- Open Evidence Graph and say "input, reasoning, output, control, receipt."

## One-Sentence Differentiators

- "Not a chatbot: an evidence-backed decision workflow."
- "Not a resume generator: it starts with what to learn and what proof to build."
- "Not a black box: every recommendation has score, gap, control and receipt
  nodes."
- "Not unsafe automation: final submission is gated to the student lead."
